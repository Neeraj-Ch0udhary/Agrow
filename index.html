The Problem

80% of farmers still grow
what their parents grew

Low margins, no market access, and zero awareness of modern alternatives trap millions of Indian farmers in poverty cycles.

😔
Low Income
Traditional wheat and rice earn ₹5,000–8,000/month. Modern crops earn 3–5× more in the same space and time.
📚
No Awareness
Most farmers have never heard of mushroom farming, microgreens, hydroponics, or medicinal plants — let alone how to grow them.
🛒
No Market Access
Farmers sell at mandi rates. Selling directly to restaurants pays 40–60% more — but farmers don't know how to reach buyers.
🌱
No Growing Support
Once a farmer starts a new crop, there is no daily guidance, pest detection, or expert help readily available.

Features

Everything a farmer needs.
Nothing they don't.

🤖
AI Farming Assistant
Powered by Groq Llama 3.1 — answers farming questions in both English and Hindi. Farming-focused, always accurate, always relevant to Indian agriculture.
Groq Llama 3.1
📈
Live Mandi Prices
Real-time crop prices from 3,000+ Indian mandis via the official Agmarknet API. Filter by state, crop, and market.
data.gov.in API
🔍
Disease Detector
Photograph a sick plant and get instant AI diagnosis — disease name, severity badge, treatment steps, and prevention tips.
Llama 4 Scout Vision
🏪
Real Marketplace
Farmers post harvest listings. Buyers browse and call directly. No middlemen. RLS-secured — only owners can edit their listings.
Supabase + RLS
✅
Daily Checklist
Auto-generated daily farming tasks based on the farmer's active crop and day number in the growing cycle.
Smart Automation
🌐
Bilingual — EN + हिंदी
Complete English and Hindi support across all screens using i18next. Designed for all farmers regardless of literacy level.
